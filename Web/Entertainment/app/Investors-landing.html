From the CEO’s desk

In its new avatar, Zee would single mindedly pursue excellence in all aspects of business operations. Our teams remain committed to delight consumers by creating extraordinary entertainment experiences for viewers across platforms. As we chase our target of becoming clear leaders in each of the verticals we operate in, we remain absolutely mindful of delivering this growth profitably and with a clear focus on cash generation. We would strive hard to meet investors’ expectations on governance and disclosures and set new standards going forward. Today, with these initiatives we are laying the foundation of ZEE 4.0 and as we go along, we will continue to build on this strong foundation.

Puneet Goenka

Managing Director & CEO
