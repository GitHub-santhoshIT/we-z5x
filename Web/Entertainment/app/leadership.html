Our Leadership

Walking the talk

Our leaders are the north to our compass. Their experience, diversity, commitment, passion and determination form the lifeblood of all we do and all we aspire to do. And it shows. In our performance, our business goals, our open culture and the willingness to take risks.

person name

Subhash Chandra

Chairman Emeritus
Background
  • Educationist, philanthropist & a parliamentarian (Member of Rajya Sabha)
  • Rightly referred to as ‘The Father of Indian Television’ as his pioneering vision gifted India, not just its first private satellite television company but an entire industry in itself
  • Chairman of Ekal Global, which works for the underprivileged children, especially in the tribal areas
  • Long-standing associations with various NGOs including TALEEM Foundation, Ekal Vidyalaya, Global Vipassana Foundation, Global Foundation for Civilisation Harmony and Subhash Chandra Foundation
Recognition
  • 2011 International Emmy Directorate Award at their 39th International Emmy Awards in New York; first Indian ever to receive a Directorate Award recognising excellence in television programming outside the US
