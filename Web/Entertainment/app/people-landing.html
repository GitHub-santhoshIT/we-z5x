Our culture

Why ZEE is where you want to be

Intrinsic to our ability to tell authentic stories is people and the connections we make with them. As colleagues. As entertainers. As partners to communities. To enable and sustain this, we want to attract the best minds by giving them the personal and professional support they need. After all, connections must begin at home.

Learning & development

Extraordinary adventures. Extraordinary ideas. Endless learning.

Our version of the perfect workplace is not fancy office grounds and glitzy parties. We do have all of that, just in case. But as employers, we want to be the enablers that inspire our people to grow their careers and pursue bold, ambitious and audacious goals with us.

Learn more

The more diverse we are, the better we can be

The world evolves when we broaden our horizons and ready our minds for change. We have an exciting and exceptional combination of women and men in the system, helping make ZEE a more equitable, balanced and fulfilling workplace. Our women show us what it means to be tough, decisive and competitive at the workplace; and we are proud of the unstoppable girl power that courses through ZEE.
