ZEE 4.0:
The next big leap

The world around us is changing dramatically. Disruptions are bursting on the scene almost every day. And the rules of entertainment are being rewritten. At ZEE, we strongly believe that it is not the strongest or the toughest that survive the tide of time, but those that are most responsive to change and most adaptive to the needs of the future. And ZEE 4.0 is the future.

It is the start of a new story. One that will see us transform in line with the rapidly changing world and its expectations. One that will see us grow into a leaner, greener and sharper version of ZEE. One where we’re at the top of our game, doing what we do best. Entertaining.
