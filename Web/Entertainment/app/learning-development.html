Learning & development

Extraordinary adventures.
Extraordinary ideas.
Endless learning.

Our people are among the best and brightest in the industry, ready to drive the impact we envision ZEE to make on the global entertainment industry. And we’re invested in their growth and development to ensure they can be better than yesterday.

ZEEcademy

We have partnered with Edcast, an AI-based knowledge cloud that specialises in creating and curating content for learners from across diverse platforms to provide best-in-industry learning modules.

Ivy colleges brought home

The M&E landscape is evolving and our people must keep pace with the transformation. We help them upgrade their skills through courses from world renowned universities for free. To this end, Coursera, an online training management platform is one of our partners.

Coursera provides access to over 3,000 courses from top tier universities like Princeton, Harvard and INSEAD. Self-paced, global and are delivered by experts, these modules will open doors to an exciting world of possibilities for you. For screenwriting Alan Sorkin, the creator of Social Network and Shonda Rhimes, the show-runner of Grey’s Anatomy moderate the sessions.

EMBARK

One of our most ambitious projects to ensure personal and professional, EMBARK is shaping change agents. It is a customised module for the growth of frontline managers, encompassing engagement workshops, blended learning and sustained practical applications, and behavioural and technical skill development frameworks.

Networking made
personal

Our employees learn most when they fraternise with the best in the industry. We are enabling them to share knowledge and understand best practices from the expansive market. DesignYatra was one of our endeavours to grow marketing knowledge amongst our creative team.
