  • FAQs
    How long has the Company been in business?
    The Company was originally incorporated as Empire Holdings Ltd on November 25, 1982. In 1992, the Company entered the business of entertainment software and the name was changed to Zee Telefilms Limited with effect from September 8, 1992. Since then, the Company has expanded its operations considerably. In 2007, post de-merger of News and Cable Business Undertaking, the name of the Company was changed to Zee Entertainment Enterprises Limited (ZEEL) with effect from January 10, 2007.
