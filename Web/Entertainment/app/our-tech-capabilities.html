Our tech capabilities

On the cutting edge 
of innovation

Ensuring uninterrupted entertainment across multiple platforms is our state-of-the-art technology, our experienced programming team, our subject matter experts and our world-class content production and distribution infrastructure.

Broadcasting operations

The nerve centre of ZEE’s programming

We maintain one of the most capable automation-based media architecture that receives, processes and delivers myriad content to 150+ countries. Our broadcasting system is underpinned by five different satellites, a highly sophisticated storage mechanism and best-in-class network engines. The system helps facilitate seamless transfers of original programs, live content and news bulletins, and new movies, as well as old mastered content, films, commercials and news programs dating back to over a decade.

Broadcasting management

Mission-critical support for traffic & scheduling

Our homegrown broadcast management system supervises media traffic and scheduling across the globe. At the same time, we work closely with our air time sales teams to maximise revenues by offering a varied spread of commercials, while creating new revenue streams like contextual ad insertion, ad replacement for local markets, leading and lagging commercials, and others. The system also integrates aspects of content management, ad sales and marketing, besides ensuring compliance with applicable laws and protecting viewer and advertising interests.

Media operations

The one-stop shop for all of ZEE’s content

Key to our consumer obsession, our media processing centre is the largest of its kind in Asia, complete with state-of-the-art facilities for R&D, studio operations and digital asset management. The centre plays an important role in supporting our experts to discover new ways for strengthening the content value chain and to apply new technologies for enriching the user experience.
