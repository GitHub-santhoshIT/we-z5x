A global
content
company.

From giving India its first private satellite TV channel in 1992 to reaching 1.3 billion viewers around the world today, in 11 languages and multiple entertainment platforms. The rise of ZEE as the global entertainment go-to, with offerings across TV, digital, movies, music and live entertainment has been truly extraordinary.

Our purpose

To create
the extraordinary

We exist to entertain. The stories we tell enthral, empower and engage our audiences every day, connecting with their souls and their complex yet intriguing world. In everything we do, we dare to not only be better than before, but also bigger and bolder. Because we aspire to be extraordinary, not as a goal, but as the standard. Because the extra mile is where the magic happens.
